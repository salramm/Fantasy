Home Town

Located on a beautiful Honolulu Island Honolulu Basketball Club is an up-and-coming Team that is quickly building up its very loyal fan base.

Home Court

Honolulu BC is still playing on the same court the team was founded on in 2018. Stay tuned for the new stadium that is currently under construction, Scheduled to complete by the end of 2021

2021 Season Jerseys have become a hit with Honolulu fans. The team plans to add more designs to their inventory to celebrate their victory. The uniforms will also change their look for the coming season as league’s champions.

Manager Profile

Tom Rabbit

The team’s manager became a hero in the hearts of every Honolulu resident. The city has commissioned a monument to Mr. Bewkes for his dedication to the team’s success. The word on the street is that Mr. Bewkes is the reason “Tom” became the most popular name for newborn babies since the founding of the city.
