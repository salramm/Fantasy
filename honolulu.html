Manager Profile

Tom Rabbit

The team’s manager became a hero in the hearts
of every Honolulu resident. The city has commissioned
a monument to Mr. Rabbit for his dedication to
the team’s success. The word on the street is that Mr. Rabbit
is the reason “Tom” became the most popular name for newborn
babies since the founding of the city.
